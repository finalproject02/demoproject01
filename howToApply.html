How to Apply

Candidates are advised to collect application forms from the Admission office on payment of TK. 500/- for undergraduate and TK. 500/- for graduate programs and submit the filled-up form along with 4 copies passport size photographs and attested photocopies of all certificates, transcripts and testimonials with the deadline.

Undergraduate Graduate:

Admission Procedure
How to pay

Minimum Requirements for Undergraduate Program

1. Minimum score of GPA 2.50 both in SSC & HSC exam or CGPA 6.00 for both exam in aggregate & not less than 2.00 in any SSC & HSC .
2. Five subjects in O Level & two subjects in A level and Grade B in at least four subjects and Grade C in the remaining subjects.

Minimum Requirements for MBA(Regular)

1. Graduate in any discipline
2. Having at least 08(Eight) points

Minimum Requirements for MBA(Executive)

1. Graduate in any discipline
2. Minimum 2(two) years work experience in executive position

Admission Procedure

1. Meet Admission Officer for advising
2. Buy Admission Form and Prospectus
3. Fill in the Admission Form

Submit Admission Form with following documents

1. S.S.C Certificate
2. H.S.C Certificate
3. Graduate Certificate for Post Graduate Programme
4. Marks / Grade Sheet
5. Four copies of passport size and one copy of stamp size photographs.
6. On the offer of admission, pay (shown on Pay – in- Slip) to South East Bank.
7. Submit filled – in Registration Form for registration.

How to pay

The students who deserve to get admission to this university have to pay all type of fees to the following designation:
♦ At University Campus National Bank Booth (2nd floor of Building A) Or
♦ National Bank (Laldighirpar branch)
