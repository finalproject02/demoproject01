Frequently Ask Questions for Admission at Leading University

The Admission information is available in the following places:
i) LU website (www.lus.ac.bd) .
ii) The Admission Desk, LU (Cell: +8801755-84164, +8801976871188) One can also get Admission information by sending queries at the e-mail address: Admissions@lus.ac.bd

LU Offers 3 semesters a year: Spring, Summer & Fall, and these starts in January, May & September respectively for BBA, ENG, LL.B, Islamic Studies, CSE, EEE. For Architecture, Civil Engineering, Bachelor of Tourism and Hospitality Management and Bangla. LU Offers Admission for these department twice a year. You can apply in either Spring or Summer semester. The classes start from January and July accordingly.
